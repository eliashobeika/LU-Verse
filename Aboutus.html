the website's mission

The Lebanese University can be challenging and fast-paced, so we, as non-affiliated students, decided to create a welcoming and user-friendly website to support our peers. This platform will serve as a central hub for news, updates, coursework, and more, making it easier to stay organized and connected. Our goal is to make your stay at the university a bit easier and help you learn and grow. Thank you for using our website!
